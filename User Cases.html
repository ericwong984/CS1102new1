Customer Service

AR technology creates virtual tour guides, allowing clients to receive guidance and services remotely.

  • The National Bank of Oman provides AR services that help clients find the nearest ATM or branch.

Employee training

Virtual environments simulate real-world banking scenarios, training staff on customer service, fraud detection, or compliance efficiently and cost-effectively.

  • Bank of America launched a VR training program in nearly 4,300 financial centers.
  • Allowing around 50,000 employees to practice complex tasks and client interactions. .

Events

Banks host virtual conferences, product launches, or investor meetings in the Metaverse, enabling global participation and engaging experiences without physical venues.

  • JPMorgan Chase held an event called DEVUP 2024.
  • Used Metaverse technology to allow college graduates to interact with different senior executives from global branches.

Education

VR/Metaverse platforms provide immersive financial literacy programs, using interactive simulations to teach budgeting, investing, or loan management, enhancing customer understanding.

Financial advisory

VR offers personalized, face-to-face advisory sessions in virtual branches.

  • Allowing clients to interactively explore investment options or retirement plans
  • Improving trust and accessibility.
