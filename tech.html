Technology Deep Dive

VR

Key Technology in VR Banking

Blockchain

Secures digital transactions and assets, ensuring trust in virtual environments

AI

Personalizes financial advice, detects fraud, and powers virtual assistants

5G

Enables fast, low-latency connections for seamless VR experiences

Haptic feedback

Simulates touch, enhancing interactions like signing virtual documents.

Hardware

Oculus Rift

Early VR headset for immersive banking simulations.

Meta Quest Pro

Advanced VR headset with enhanced visuals and tracking for virtual branches.

AR glasses

Overlay financial data in real-world settings, ideal for mobile banking.

Future development and prediction

Decentralized finance (DeFi) in the Metaverse

This will grow to $37 billion 2028, enabling virtual banking, trading, and AI-driven financial tools with 5G support. MetaFi will standardize assets for cross-platform use.

Ethical debates

Ethical debates arise: decentralized digital identities risk privacy, AI biases may skew services, and unregulated virtual economies face fraud concerns. By 2030, DeFi could transform banking, but resolving privacy and regulatory challenges is critical.
